Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of my white water rafting website is to get more business. I wil get this done by letting My audience discover how rafting is indeed a sociable outdoor activity that combines fun, action and sports all in one, with the added bonus of it taking place in striking natural landscapes. Roaring white water rivers, picturesque mountain panoramas and thrilling rapids turn every rafting tour into a true adventure. I would like to make the website a place where customer can by overviewing feel the coming adventure and also get basics information about enrollement and plannig.

Audience

The target customers are people of every age save under 5 years old. Since very little prior experience is needed, white water rafting is ideal for families and groups with different physical abilities as long as you seek new adeventure. Participants should have basic swimming skills. In addition, it is required that all people who want to join a white water rafting tour are in overall good health. People should be able to access the website with any device.
